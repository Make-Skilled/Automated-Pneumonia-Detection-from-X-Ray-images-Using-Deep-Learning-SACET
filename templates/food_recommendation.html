Why Nutrition Matters?

Proper nutrition plays a crucial role in recovering from pneumonia. The right foods can help strengthen the immune system, reduce inflammation, and support lung function. When fighting an infection, your body requires more energy and nutrients to heal properly.

Staying hydrated, eating nutrient-dense foods, and avoiding inflammatory foods can accelerate recovery. A well-balanced diet provides essential vitamins, minerals, and proteins necessary for lung health and overall well-being.
